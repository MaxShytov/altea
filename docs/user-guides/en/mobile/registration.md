# Creating Your Altea Account

Welcome to Altea! This guide will walk you through creating your account and getting started with your recovery journey.

## Before You Begin

Make sure you have:
- A valid email address you can access
- A strong password (at least 8 characters)
- Read and agreed to our Terms of Service and Privacy Policy

## Step-by-Step Registration

### Step 1: Open the App

Launch the Altea app on your device. If you're new, you'll see the welcome screen with options to sign in or create an account.

### Step 2: Tap "Create Account"

On the welcome screen, tap the **Create Account** button to begin registration.

### Step 3: Enter Your Information

Fill in the registration form with the following details:

| Field | Description | Requirements |
|-------|-------------|--------------|
| Email | Your email address | Must be valid and not already registered |
| First Name | Your first name | Required |
| Last Name | Your last name | Required |
| Password | Your account password | Minimum 8 characters |
| Confirm Password | Re-enter your password | Must match password |

### Step 4: Review Legal Documents

Before completing registration, you must agree to our legal documents:

1. Tap **Terms of Service** to read our terms
2. Tap **Privacy Policy** to understand how we protect your data
3. Check the box to confirm you agree

> **Important:** We take your privacy seriously. Your health data is encrypted and protected under Swiss and EU data protection laws.

### Step 5: Create Your Account

Tap the **Create Account** button. If all information is correct, you'll see a confirmation screen.

### Step 6: Verify Your Email

After registration, we'll send a verification email to your address:

1. Open your email inbox
2. Look for an email from **Altea** (check spam/junk if not in inbox)
3. Click the **Verify Email** button in the email
4. You'll see a confirmation page in your browser
5. Tap **Open Altea App** to return to the app

> **Note:** The verification link expires after 24 hours. If it expires, you can request a new one.

## Troubleshooting

### I didn't receive the verification email

1. Check your spam/junk folder
2. Make sure you entered the correct email address
3. Wait a few minutes - emails can sometimes be delayed
4. Request a new verification email from the app

### The verification link expired

1. Open the Altea app
2. You'll see an option to resend the verification email
3. Check your inbox for the new email
4. Click the new verification link

### "Email already registered" error

This means an account already exists with your email. Try:
1. Sign in with your existing account
2. Use "Forgot Password" if you don't remember your password
3. Contact support if you believe this is an error

### Password doesn't meet requirements

Your password must:
- Be at least 8 characters long
- Not be too similar to your email or name
- Not be a commonly used password
- Not be entirely numeric

Try using a mix of letters, numbers, and special characters.
