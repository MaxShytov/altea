# Registration FAQ

Frequently asked questions about creating an Altea account.

---

## Account Creation

### Q: What do I need to create an account?

**A:** You need:
- A valid email address
- A password (minimum 8 characters)
- To be at least 18 years old
- To accept our Terms of Service and Privacy Policy

---

### Q: Is Altea free to use?

**A:** Yes, creating an account and using core features is free. Premium features may be available in the future.

---

### Q: Can I use a work email address?

**A:** Yes, you can use any valid email address. However, we recommend using a personal email for privacy, as Altea handles sensitive health information.

---

### Q: Why do I need to verify my email?

**A:** Email verification:
- Confirms you own the email address
- Protects your account from unauthorized access
- Enables password recovery
- Allows us to send important notifications

---

## Password Requirements

### Q: What are the password requirements?

**A:** Your password must:
- Be at least 8 characters long
- Not be too similar to your email or name
- Not be a commonly used password (like "password123")
- Not be entirely numeric

---

### Q: Why was my password rejected?

**A:** Common reasons:
- **Too short:** Must be at least 8 characters
- **Too common:** Passwords like "qwerty123" are blocked
- **Too similar to your info:** Don't use your name or email in your password
- **All numbers:** Add some letters

**Tip:** Use a mix of uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and symbols.

---

### Q: Can I see my password while typing?

**A:** Yes, tap the eye icon next to the password field to show/hide your password.

---

## Email Verification

### Q: I didn't receive the verification email. What should I do?

**A:** Try these steps:
1. **Check spam/junk folder** - Emails sometimes get filtered
2. **Wait 5 minutes** - Email delivery can be delayed
3. **Check the email address** - Make sure you entered it correctly
4. **Request a new email** - Use the "Resend Verification" option in the app

---

### Q: How long is the verification link valid?

**A:** The verification link expires after **24 hours**. If it expires, request a new one from the app.

---

### Q: Can I request multiple verification emails?

**A:** Yes, but there's a limit of 3 requests per hour to prevent abuse. Each new email invalidates the previous link.

---

### Q: The verification link doesn't work. What should I do?

**A:** The link might be:
- **Expired:** Request a new verification email
- **Already used:** Try signing in - your email might already be verified
- **Broken:** Copy the full URL manually into your browser

---

## Account Issues

### Q: I get "Email already registered" error. What does this mean?

**A:** An account with this email already exists. You can:
1. **Sign in** with your existing account
2. **Reset password** if you forgot it
3. **Contact support** if you didn't create the account

---

### Q: Can I change my email after registration?

**A:** Currently, you cannot change your email address. If needed, contact support for assistance.

---

### Q: Can I have multiple accounts?

**A:** We recommend having only one account per person for the best experience. Multiple accounts are not officially supported.

---

### Q: I registered but can't log in. Why?

**A:** Check if:
1. **Email is verified** - You must verify before logging in
2. **Password is correct** - Use "Forgot Password" if unsure
3. **Email is correct** - Check for typos
4. **App is updated** - Use the latest version

---

## Legal & Privacy

### Q: Why do I have to accept Terms of Service?

**A:** It's a legal requirement. The Terms explain:
- How you can use the app
- Your rights and responsibilities
- Important health disclaimers

---

### Q: What happens to my data when I register?

**A:** Your data is:
- Encrypted and stored securely
- Protected under Swiss and EU privacy laws
- Never sold to third parties
- Under your control (you can export or delete it)

---

### Q: Can I register without accepting the Privacy Policy?

**A:** No, accepting the Privacy Policy is required because we need your consent to process health-related data. This is a legal requirement under GDPR and Swiss FADP.

---

## Technical Issues

### Q: Registration is stuck on loading. What should I do?

**A:** Try:
1. Check your internet connection
2. Close and reopen the app
3. Wait a moment and try again
4. If the problem persists, contact support

---

### Q: I get a "Rate limit exceeded" error. What does this mean?

**A:** Too many registration attempts from your device. This is a security feature. Wait 1 hour and try again, or contact support if this seems like an error.

---

### Q: The app crashes when I try to register. What should I do?

**A:**
1. Update to the latest app version
2. Restart your device
3. Try again
4. If it continues, contact support with your device model and OS version
