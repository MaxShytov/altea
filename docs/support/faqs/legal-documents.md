# Legal Documents FAQ

Frequently asked questions about Terms of Service, Privacy Policy, and your data rights.

---

## Terms of Service

### Q: What is covered in the Terms of Service?

**A:** Our Terms of Service cover:
- Eligibility requirements (18+ years)
- How you can use Altea
- Account security responsibilities
- Health disclaimers
- Intellectual property
- Limitation of liability
- Governing law (Switzerland)

---

### Q: Why is there a health disclaimer?

**A:** Altea is a **wellness support tool**, not a medical device. The disclaimer clarifies that:
- Altea doesn't diagnose or treat medical conditions
- You should consult healthcare professionals for medical advice
- AI-generated insights are suggestions, not medical recommendations

---

### Q: What law governs Altea's terms?

**A:** Swiss law governs our Terms of Service. Disputes are handled in Zurich, Switzerland courts. EU consumers retain protections under their local consumer laws.

---

### Q: Can Altea terminate my account?

**A:** Yes, but only if you:
- Violate the Terms of Service
- Engage in fraudulent activity
- Pose a security risk

You'll be notified if your account is terminated, and you can request your data.

## Privacy & Data Protection

### Q: What data does Altea collect?

**A:** We collect:

| Category | Examples |
|----------|----------|
| Account data | Email, name, password |
| Health data | Recovery tracking, assessments, mood logs |
| Technical data | Device type, app usage, crash reports |

---

### Q: Is my health data safe?

**A:** Yes. Your health data is:
- **Encrypted** in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest (AES-256)
- **Classified as sensitive** under Swiss and EU law
- **Never sold** to anyone
- **Not used for advertising**

---

### Q: Who can see my data?

**A:**
- **You** have full access to your data
- **Healthcare providers** only if you explicitly share with them
- **Support staff** only when you contact support (limited access)
- **No one else** - we don't sell or share your data

---

### Q: Does Altea use AI on my data?

**A:** Yes, for features like:
- Personalized insights
- Pattern recognition
- Assessment analysis

**Important:** When AI processes your data, it's **anonymized first**. No human reviews your personal data unless you contact support.

---

### Q: What laws protect my data?

**A:**
- **Swiss FADP (DSG)** - Swiss Federal Act on Data Protection (2023)
- **EU GDPR** - General Data Protection Regulation
- Both classify health data as requiring **special protection**

---

## Your Rights

### Q: What rights do I have over my data?

**A:** Under Swiss and EU law, you have the right to:

| Right | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Access** | Get a copy of all your data |
| **Rectification** | Correct inaccurate data |
| **Erasure** | Delete your data ("right to be forgotten") |
| **Portability** | Export data in machine-readable format |
| **Restriction** | Limit how we process your data |
| **Object** | Object to certain processing |
| **Withdraw consent** | Stop health data processing |

---

### Q: How do I export my data?

**A:**
1. Go to **Settings** > **Privacy** > **Export Data**
2. Choose format (JSON or PDF)
3. Your data will be prepared and sent to your email

---

### Q: How do I delete my account and data?

**A:**
1. Go to **Settings** > **Account** > **Delete Account**
2. Confirm your decision
3. Your data will be deleted within 30 days

**Note:** Some data may be retained if required by law.

---

### Q: Can I withdraw consent for health data processing?

**A:** Yes. Go to **Settings** > **Privacy** > **Manage Consent**.

**Important:** Withdrawing consent means you can no longer use health tracking features, as we need consent to process this data legally.

## Sharing Data

### Q: Can I share my progress with my doctor?

**A:** Yes! The sharing feature lets you:
1. Choose what data to share
2. Generate a secure link
3. Optionally add password protection
4. Share the link with your healthcare provider
5. Revoke access at any time

---

### Q: Is shared data secure?

**A:** Yes. Shared links:
- Can be password-protected
- Have optional expiration dates
- Can be revoked at any time
- Are encrypted in transit

---

### Q: What happens to shared data if I delete my account?

**A:** When you delete your account:
- All shared links stop working immediately
- Healthcare providers lose access
- Shared data is deleted

## Complaints & Issues

### Q: How do I report a privacy concern?

**A:** Contact our privacy team:
- **Email:** privacy@altea.ch
- **Response time:** Within 30 days (legally required)

---

### Q: Where can I file a formal complaint?

**A:** You can file complaints with:

**Switzerland:**
- Federal Data Protection Commissioner (FDPIC)
- Website: www.edoeb.admin.ch

**EU:**
- Your local Data Protection Authority

---

### Q: I think my data was breached. What should I do?

**A:**
1. **Contact us immediately:** privacy@altea.ch
2. **Change your password**
3. **Monitor for suspicious activity**

If we confirm a breach affecting your data, we'll:
- Notify you directly
- Explain what happened
- Tell you what steps we're taking
